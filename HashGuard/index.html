Vol Lite

Preserve the integrity and existence of digital files through cryptographic timestamping.

A cryptographic hash creates a unique fingerprint of a file. By anchoring this fingerprint on a blockchain, it becomes possible to prove that a document existed at a specific moment in time.

Whether you want to prove the existence of an important document, protect intellectual property, verify the authenticity of a file, or keep a permanent record of information, blockchain timestamping provides a way to create a trusted proof of existence.
